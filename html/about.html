How to Play

  1. Get a device with internet access
    • Phone
    • Tablet
    • Laptop
  2. Go to jackbox.tv on your favorite browser
  3. Enter the room code displayed on the game screen
    • It is a four letter code
  4. Enter a nickname
    • Nicknames must be 12 characters or less
    • In some games, it may be important to have a recognizable name
  5. Wait for the host to start the game
  6. Follow the prompts and instructions on the TV and your device to play!
